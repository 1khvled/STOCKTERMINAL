What does DuPont ROE mean? ROE (Return on Equity) measures operational efficiency across three core drivers:
  • Profitability (Net Profit Margin): Profit generated per $1 of sales.
  • Asset Efficiency (Asset Turnover): Asset productivity in generating sales.
  • Leverage (Equity Multiplier): Equity multiplier relative to debt.

What does the Altman Z-Score mean? The Altman Z-Score compiles 5 crucial financial ratios (measuring liquidity, profitability, productivity, leverage, and activity) to evaluate financial stability.
Safe Zone (Z > 2.99) Excellent financial strength. Virtually no immediate distress risk.
Grey Zone (1.81 - 2.99) Moderate risk. Leverage or margin pressure. Monitor closely.
Distress Zone (Z < 1.81) High risk. Serious financial pressure and debt obligations.
